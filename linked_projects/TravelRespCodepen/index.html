New York City, New York

New York City, also known as The Big Apple, is divided into five areas – Brooklyn, Queens, Staten Island, the Bronx, and the best-known of all, Manhattan.This is where you will find most of the city's top attractions, such as the Empire State Building, Central Park, Times Square, the Chrysler Building and more.

Chicago Illinois

Some of the many things Chicago is famous for are: Chicago-style hot dogs, Chicago-style (deep dish) pizza, Maxwell Street Polish Sausage, jazz music, and 1920s gangsters like Al Capone. Chicago is also known for interesting architecture like the Sears Tower, many museums, and many loyal sports fans..

Los Angeles, California

Los Angeles, also called the city of angels,is located in Southern California and is the center of the united states film and television industry. Close to the famous Hollywood sign, studios corporations such as Paramount Pictures, Universal and Warner Brotherss offer behind-the-scenes tours. On Hollywood Boulevard, TCL Chinese Theatre displays celebrities’ hand- and footprints, the Walk of Fame honors thousands of luminaries.
