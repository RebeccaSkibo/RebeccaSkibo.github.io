Packing for New York

New York can be very cold during the fall and winter months, so it is a good idea to pack warm clothes, and always pack extras! Here are things that would be a good idea to pack for your trip to new york

  • 2 Warm Wool or fleece had
  • Wool or cotton lined now boots, preferrably water proof
  • Jeans, warm pants
  • Two layered coats
  • three pairs of socks for every day your there. You don't want to pack one pair of socks for each day and have wet socks or runout
  • Sweaters
  • Scarves
  • Gloves, wool or cotton, as air shouldn't get through
  • Warm Pajamas
  • cozy slippers
  • Undershirts

Packing For Chicago

The things you'll need to pack for chiago are quite similar to those you'd pack for New York, as both can become incredibly cold during the fall and winter months.

  • 2 Warm Wool or fleece hat
  • Water proof wool or cotton lined snow boots.
  • warm pants
  • Two layered coats
  • Long socks
  • Sweaters
  • Scarves
  • Gloves, wool or cotton, as air shouldn't get through
  • Warm Pajamas
  • cozy slippers
  • Undershirts
  • An appetite, they have incredible pizza!

What to pack for Los Angeles

Unlike our two previous destination, The city of angels does not get bitter cold during the winter months. The best time to visit is during october, as the temperatures stay in the 60's and 70's, although there will be rain. Here is what to pack for your trip to la!

  • Dresses
  • Jeans
  • Leggings
  • Tank tops
  • Sneakers/tennis shoes
  • Tee shirts
  • Umbrellas!
  • Jacket for the evening
  • Sunscreen! You can still get a burn in the winter months!

Why What you pack is important

Being Prepared and knowing what to pack for your trip is Crucial to smooth sailing. If you pack summer clothes for a destination that is going to be slightly cold, you're more likely to buy new clothes, which can be expensive depending on where you are. Always make a plan of what to pack and check what the weather is like during the time your trip is going to take place.

how's the weather over there?

Make sure you research the area you are visiting. Is it warm during the month you're going? Does it have swimming holes? Should you pack extra money for a ticket to local attractions? Make a check list of things you should know before you go!
